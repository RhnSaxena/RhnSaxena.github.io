Inter IIIT

This year PDPM IIITDM Jabalpur will be hosting the Inter-IIIT Sports Meet as it's annual Sports Fest : GUSTO. This will be the fourth edition in the series with the first three being successfully hosted by IIITDM Kancheepuram, ABV-IIITM Gwalior and IIIT Allahabad. With participants from over 25 IIITs from all over India, it is one of the largest fests in Central India.

GUSTO 2020

GUSTO – the Annual Sports Fest of IIITDM Jabalpur held every year during the even semester where the sports enthusiasts of various colleges come together into the Institute campus to showcase their talents and participate in the competitive sporting events. This year GUSTO gives you the opportunity to grow as a sports person inculcated with various values and provides a healthy competitive environment which will be full of excitement and joy.
